Instructions

The graph visualizes the correlation of the range of income distribution and the attendance of various college tiers. The data may be very clustered but by uing the interactive tool on the embedded Tableau graph one can see certain trends. The x and y axises graph the probability of the childrens' possible income quintile based on the parents' income quintile and the scatterplots are organized by college tier names.
